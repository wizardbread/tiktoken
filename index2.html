      Tik Token, originally conceived as a meme coin with a max supply of 1, has transformed into a utility token with a unique reward structure and interesting value proposition. By linking social media identities with Web3 wallets, Tik Token simplifies crypto transactions and aims to drive mainstream adoption. We'll explore the features of Tik Token, the tokenomics, its roadmap, and its potential to revolutionize the way we transact in the crypto space.

WHAT ITS ALL ABOUT!







Mint TikToken


Get rewarded by linking your TikTok account with your Web3 wallet. You’ll earn more TikToken based on how many followers you have on TikTok. But don’t mint too late, once halving occurs it’ll be 10x harder to mint the same amount of TIK!





Tiktokenomics


TikToken has unique tokenomics. Firstly, it has a max supply of 1, meaning the asset price will be greater than or equal to the market cap of the token. Not only that but the amount you own is equal to the percentage you own, represented as a decimal value of the max supply. Ie. If you have 0.01 TIK that`s equivalent to owning 1%. We also employ a unique halving cycle, where remaining supply is cut in half the reward is cut by a factor of 10 to ensure the longevity of the network. This means that as time goes on the wealthiest owners have the fewest 0s unlike traditional finance where more 0s usually mean you have more.


The Halving Cycle


There will be 13 halving cycles, the first one starts at 0.4096 TIK when rewards get reduced from 0.00001 (0.₄₋1) TIK to 0.000001 (0.₅₋1) TIK per 1000 followers (rounded up). This carries on until the last halving cycle the last one at 0.0001 TIK where rewards drop to 0.000000000000000001 (0.₁₇₋1) TIK per 1000 followers. During the last halving cycle, rewards are rounded down per 1000 followers instead of rounded up Here`s a list of all halvings and the total follower count to get to the next halving.

RoadMap


  • Road Map:
    • Launch Beta API and API viewer
    • Enlist up to 100 developers to use the name resolution service in their Web3 platforms.
    • Create and deploy a visual way to represent the database
    • Deadline: July 14, 2023.



  • Integration of Additional Platforms:
    • Integrate a new platform every week, starting with TikTok.
    • Platforms to integrate: Twitter, YouTube/Google, Instagram, Telegram, Reddit, Discord, and GitHub.
    • Address individual approval processes and challenges.
    • Ensure compatibility and modify existing resolver modules.
    • Engage with platforms' communities and development teams.
    • Deadline: August 1, continual



  • Create V2 Smart Contract:
    • Add in the default wallet, account signing and governance
    • Modify the tokenomics based on learning lessons from the initial drop.
    • Fix liquidity issues while still locking tokens.
    • Validate and test the contract on test net while completing security audits.
    • Deadline: September 2023



  • Fork Major Open-Source Hot Wallets:
    • Fork MetaMask and Trust Wallet.
    • Identify existing resolver modules and add your own.
    • Incorporate basic features for sending crypto to social media handles.
    • Establish communication with the development teams.
    • Ensure security and open the code for auditing.
    • Deadline: November 1, 2023.



  • Announce and Launch Beta dApp (WebPort):
    • Provide a unified experience across web1, web2, and Web3.
    • Enable non-custodial and self-custody wallets.
    • Link existing self-custody wallets (e.g., MetaMask and Trust Wallet).
    • Include self-custody blue checkmark system, KYC integration, and tipping features.
    • Provide user support and documentation.
    • Ensure security and privacy of user data.
    • Deadline: March 2023 until further notice.



  • Drop Beta API/WebPort and Go Fully Public:
    • Transition from beta to fully public version of WebPort dApp.
    • Collect user feedback and implement improvements.
    • Develop a marketing strategy to attract users.
    • Introduce a new premium API with advanced integration capabilities.
    • Expand team or seek partnerships as needed.
    • Measure success and user satisfaction.
    • Deadline: Q3 2024.



  • Domain name integration
    • Incorporate TLDs as a bridge between Web1, Web2 and Web3.
    • Learn about becoming a registrar and what it would take to use standard domains.
    • Deadline: 2025
